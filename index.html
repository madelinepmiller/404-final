About Me

Hi, I'm Suzanne Collins, the author of The Hunger Games trilogy. My journey to becoming a successful author began with my love for storytelling. After studying theater and telecommunications at Indiana University, I worked as a writer for children's television shows, which helped me develop my writing skills. Inspired by my father's experiences in the Vietnam War, I began writing my own stories, which eventually led me to create The Hunger Games. The series quickly became a global phenomenon, and it was incredibly humbling to see readers connect with the vivid characters, intricate plot, and themes of power, freedom, and rebellion. Today, I'm honored to be recognized as a pioneering force in young adult literature, and I'm grateful for the opportunity to continue telling stories that inspire and resonate with readers around the world.
